### Hi there 👋

I am a researcher working on next-generation AI systems with a focus on **learning representations**, **interpretability**, and **compositional reasoning**, aiming to build models that are **auditable, controllable, and aligned with human values**.  

I currently serve as (1) a **Senior Research Scientist (Head of AI) at [MARSAIL](https://kaopanboonyuen.github.io/MARS/)**, (2) a **C2F Postdoctoral Fellow at [Chulalongkorn University](https://kaopanboonyuen.github.io/files/postdoc/panboonyuen_C2F_PostDoc_Experience_Certificate_2025_2026.pdf)**, and (3) an **Adjunct Professor at [Khon Kaen University](https://computing.kku.ac.th/teerapong.panboonyuen)**. I am also the founder of **[PBYAIL](https://pbylab.github.io/)**, where I develop scalable AI systems on geo-data for disaster resilience and social impact. I received my **Ph.D. in Computer Engineering** from [Chulalongkorn University](https://kaopanboonyuen.github.io/talk/ph.d.-thesis-defense/).
