I am also the founder of **[PBYAIL](https://pbylab.github.io/)**, where I develop scalable AI systems on geo-data for disaster resilience and social impact. I received my **Ph.D. in Computer Engineering** from [Chulalongkorn University](https://kaopanboonyuen.github.io/talk/ph.d.-thesis-defense/).
